Cricket Player DataBase Management Abstract: The aim of the project is to provide the complete information of the National and International Cricket statistics. The information is available Country wise, and Player wise. The project have following Master Screens. 1) Country 2) Player Details 3) Team Members 4) Matches By entering the data of the each match. We can get all type of reports instantly. Which will be useful to call back history of each player, Also the team performance in each match. We can get a report on numbers of matches win, and last. We can do Analysis of the data and we can perform multiple operations on the data like Adding / Removing a player Adding / Removing a country Getting the Average of a player Getting averages by grouping Getting max scores in every Format
